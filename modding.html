Total War: Warhammer 2 Modding

I continued development of the mods pictured above from their original authors. They required many table updates and some rewriting to work with newer versions of the game. The mods primarily feature new units with custom models, with their own stat balances. These were primrarily all developed within the same 2-3 months over the early summer of 2022. Early work began during my winter break period at the start of 2022.
